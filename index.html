Meet Betty

"Every woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and effortlessly styled."

Styled By Betty is a professional hair stylist brand based in Oyi, Anambra State — focused on premium wig installations, customization, and beauty transformations. With a passion for detail and a commitment to quality, we specialize in flawless lace installs that look and feel completely natural.

Whether you're looking for a sleek frontal, a vibrant color transformation, or a full wig revamp — we bring your vision to life with care, skill, and luxury results every time.
